# Pitch stats

Three to five real Malaysian SME stats with sources. **Cite or omit. Do not invent numbers.**

Lane D fills these in by 4 AM Sunday. Each stat must have:
- Number with unit
- One-sentence framing
- Source organisation
- Year of the figure
- URL to the source document

## Template

```
## Stat 1 — [topic]

**Figure:** RM X billion / N% / M merchants

**Framing:** "X% of Malaysian SMEs are micro-merchants. Most never apply for capital because the paperwork is in a register they do not speak."

**Source:** [Org name], [year], [URL]

**Pitch line:** "[the one-line version that lands in the deck]"
```

## Candidate sources

- AKPK (Agensi Kaunseling dan Pengurusan Kredit) — household credit research
- BNM (Bank Negara Malaysia) — Annual Report and Financial Stability Review chapters on SME financing
- DOSM (Department of Statistics Malaysia) — Economic Census, Monthly Wholesale and Retail Trade Survey
- SME Corp Malaysia — annual SME masterplan progress reports
- World Bank Malaysia Economic Monitor
